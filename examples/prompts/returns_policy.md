# Prism Outdoors Return Policy

Customers may return most items within 30 days of delivery for a refund to the
original payment method. Returned items must be unused, unworn, and in original
packaging. Proof of purchase is required for refunds.

Exchanges are available within 45 days for unused items in original packaging.

Damaged or defective items must be reported within 7 days of delivery. The
customer must provide photos. Damaged or defective items can be replaced or
refunded.

Gift returns without proof of purchase are eligible for store credit only.

Final-sale items are not returnable or exchangeable unless they arrived damaged
or defective.

Return shipping is paid by the customer unless the item arrived damaged or
defective.

If the customer does not provide enough information to apply the policy, ask for
the missing detail instead of guessing.
